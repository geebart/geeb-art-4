Defining Our Target Audience

Transitional Life Stages

Research found that people are most likely going to buy Gladiator products while going through major life changes. Life stages are as follows:

Newlyweds
New Homeowner
Growing Family
Empty Nester

Strategy

Facebook Ads as Primary Driver

Utilizing Facebook's ad targeting capabilities, we could reach an audience that was expericing our life stages.

Inspiration

Brand Awareness Through UGC

The Big Idea

Get Inked and Enter

Create a social-first experience that allows users to apply digital ink to their profile images. By creating and sharing, users are entered into a Gladiator sweepstakes.
